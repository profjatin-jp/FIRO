How do you connect with others?

This questionnaire explores your interpersonal tendencies across three dimensions: Inclusion, Control, and Affection.

For each statement, choose how often it describes you. There are no right or wrong answers.

Interpersonal Needs

Your scores across Inclusion, Control, and Affection — both how you express each need and how much you want others to meet it.

Developed for training purposes. Grounded in Schutz's (1958) FIRO theory. Not a validated psychometric instrument — scores are for reflection and discussion only.
